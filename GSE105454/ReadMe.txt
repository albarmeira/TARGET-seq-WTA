In this folder we provide metadata and raw (RPKM for full-length scRNA-seq data or counts for 3'TARGET-seq data) gene expression matrices for GEO: GSE105454 

https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE105454

corresponding to our publication Rodriguez-Meira A, et al. Unravelling Intratumoral Heterogeneity through High-Sensitivity Single-Cell Mutational Analysis and Parallel RNA Sequencing. Mol Cell 2019 Mar 21;73(6):1292-1305.e8. PMID: 30765193

Raw counts for 3'TARGET-seq dataset are not uploaded due to size constraints
